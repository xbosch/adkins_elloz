Rising Form

This work introduces tension through asymmetry. The central form stretches and bends. It resists balance. It feels organic yet unresolved. Yellow and green push against a cold blue field. Loose graphite lines interrupt the painted surface. They do not explain. They destabilize. The image reads as a sign without a meaning. A gesture without a message. This piece rewards long viewing. Its structure reveals itself slowly. It is not decorative. It is structural.
