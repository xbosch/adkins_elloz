Direction

This work introduces opposition. Two forms occupy the field without interaction. One is compact and closed. The other is vertical and directional. The triangular structure suggests ascent. Red and yellow create internal tension. The inverted base stabilizes what could otherwise fall. The circular form resists movement. It holds space rather than claiming it. Green acts as a neutral ground. It allows separation without conflict. This symbol is about coexistence. Not harmony. Not struggle. Just presence.
