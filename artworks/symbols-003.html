Crossing

This piece asserts the symbol as structure. The form is centered and expansive. It opens in four directions. It claims space. Yellow dominates with weight and confidence. The grey field holds it in suspension. Black contours enforce clarity. Loose lines cut across the surface, refusing closure. The image suggests movement without destination. It feels both architectural and bodily. Stable and agitated at the same time. This work anchors the Symbols series. It is the clearest statement of its internal logic.
