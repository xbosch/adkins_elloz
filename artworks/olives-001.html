First Light

This is the anchor piece of the series. It sets the tone and defines the language that all others follow. The form is bold and immediate. Color is unapologetic and physical. Black outlines hold the composition together with authority. The surface shows the hand. Nothing is hidden. Nothing is corrected. The diagonal ground creates tension and energy without telling a story. The image does not ask to be interpreted. It asks to be lived with. This is a statement work. Owning it means owning the beginning.
