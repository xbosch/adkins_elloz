Discharge

This work breaks the symbol apart. A central figure emerges only to dissolve. Lines radiate outward with urgency. There is no containment and no hierarchy. The surface is dense and layered. Color accumulates rather than settles. Marks overlap, collide, and cancel each other. The symbol here is not stable. It vibrates. It resists definition. This piece expands the Symbols series into motion and noise. It is the most expressive and least resolved work of the set. It carries intensity rather than clarity.
