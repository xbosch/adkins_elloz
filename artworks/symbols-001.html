Field

This work establishes the symbolic register of the series. Forms are reduced to their minimum. Triangles and blocks are repeated, rotated, and compressed. Color becomes a structural element rather than an accent. There is no center. No hierarchy. The eye moves continuously across the surface. The hand is visible. The surface is active. Marks accumulate without correction. This piece does not represent an object. It proposes a system. Owning it means entering the logic of the series at its origin.
