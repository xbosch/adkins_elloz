Gathering

This work introduces grouping. Multiple forms gather around a central core. They touch but do not merge. Each retains its own boundary. Color is used to separate roles. The purple center anchors the composition. The surrounding shapes orbit without hierarchy. The horizontal division of the background stabilizes the image. Red carries weight. Green opens space. This symbol suggests relation rather than meaning. It is about proximity. It is about balance. It is one of the most resolved pieces in the series.
