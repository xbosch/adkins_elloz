MoonLight Art Gallery

Adkins Elloz develops images through constraint. Flat color direct gesture and limited form define the practice.

There is no narrative. There is no symbolism offered as guidance. What remains is meant to be lived with not decoded.

The work holds space. It does not resolve.

Alongside painting, Adkins Elloz develops a poetry practice focused on time, material pressure, and memory as physical trace. The poems follow the same discipline as the visual work: reduction, precision, and emotional force without ornament.

Adkins Elloz lives and works in California. The practice unfolds through series, both visual and textual. Each body of work operates as a closed system built on repetition and insistence.
